<h1>🎁 Customer Sales Analysis & Dashboard</h1>
<h2>🚀 Excel-Based Business Intelligence Project</h2>
<h3>📖 Project Overview</h3>

This project provides a complete analysis of customer purchasing behavior, revenue trends, and product performance for a gifting retail business.
<br>
By combining multiple datasets (orders, customers, and products), the project uncovers key business drivers across:
<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;• Occasions 🎉
<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;• Locations 📍
<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;• Time patterns ⏰
<br>
💡 The final output is an interactive Excel dashboard designed for business decision-making.

<h3>🎯 Business Objectives</h3>
• Analyze revenue and order trends
<br>
• Identify top-performing products and categories
<br>
• Understand customer behavior across demographics
<br>
• Discover location-based and time-based sales patterns

🗄️ Data Architecture

The project integrates multiple datasets:

<h3>📄 Orders Dataset</h3>
• 1,000 transactions (date, time, location, occasion)
<br>
• 👥 Customer Dataset
<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;• 100 unique customers (gender, city, demographics)
<br>
• 🛍️ Products Dataset
<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;• 70 products (category, price in INR)
<br>
• 📊 Excel Dashboard File
<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;• Final integrated dataset + interactive dashboard
